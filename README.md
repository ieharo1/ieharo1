### 🦺 AI-Powered Safety Helmet Detection System

**Research Project:** Development of a safety helmet usage detection system aimed at improving accident prevention in industrial environments.

**Technologies:** Python, OpenCV, YOLO

**Description:**
The system uses a camera to identify in real-time whether workers comply with personal protection regulations, generating notifications in case of non-compliance. This proposal seeks to strengthen occupational safety through the integration of open-source computer vision technologies, practically combining programming knowledge, industrial maintenance and occupational safety.

**Impact:**
Provides an innovative and educational tool that allows students and professionals to explore real applications of artificial intelligence in industry, contributing to comprehensive training in the use of modern technologies and their impact on workplace safety.
